<dependencyManagement>
    <dependencies>
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.springframework</groupId>
            <artifactId>spring-webflux</artifactId>
            <version>6.1.12</version>
        </dependency>
    </dependencies>
</dependencyManagement>



You can override the version of spring-webflux directly in your pom.xml while keeping the Spring Boot parent version as is. Add a <dependencyManagement> section to enforce the version you need.


This ensures that Spring Boot uses the version of spring-webflux you specify rather than the one defined in the parent.
